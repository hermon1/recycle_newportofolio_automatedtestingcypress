About Us

'Re-cycle' is a eco-friendly guided cycling conpany that offers tours around the country of Norway.

Expert Tour Guides

Re-cycle expert tour guides have long experience and pride themselves on giving you the best kind of cycling holiday and creating memories that will last a lifetime for you and yours.

Flexible Booking

Re-cycle' will be offering you as much flexibility as possible with the current Covid-19 pandemic accuring. Reduced deposit fee possibility to change dates and destination free of charge.

Re-cycle' is now open for guided tours! In accordance with the state and FHI covid-19 pandemic guidelines are being taken in effect. We sanitize our helmets and bikes according to guidlines.
